Schedule

Algo Unlock

7th April

We will be providing the input format and the output format without any problem statement. Also, there will be around 4 sample inputs and their corresponding output and the player's objective is to guess the logic that has been used in producing the output.

Code Sprint (Round-1)

10th April

Get ready for an adrenaline-packed coding showdown! Join us for our 1st round of competitive coding event featuring 5-6 mind-bending problems. Battle it out for glory in 3 intense hours of coding brilliance. Top performers will qualify for the second round.

Code Sprint (Round-2)

12th April

Prepare for a heart-pounding coding face-off! Step into the arena for our thrilling second round of competitive coding, presenting 5-6 challenging problems. Engage in a 3-hour marathon of coding excellence and vie for victory.This is the grand finale where champions will be crowned.

Devathon Day-1

12th April

Day 1 of Devathon ignites with teams strategizing and coding fervently. As the first checkpoint looms, excitement builds. Participants showcase early prototypes and concepts, fueling anticipation. With each team's progress, the atmosphere brims with determination and camaraderie, setting the stage for an inspiring journey of innovation and collaboration.

Devathon Day-2

13th April

On Day 2 of Devathon, excitement escalates as mentors join the fray, guiding teams towards excellence. With checkpoints looming, participants refine their projects, fueled by expert advice and collaboration. As the clock ticks towards the 36-hour mark, anticipation builds. The stage is set for the final pitch, where industry experts and judges await to witness innovation at its finest.

Devathon Day-3

14th April

Day 3 of Devathon unfolds with teams eagerly preparing to present their groundbreaking ideas. Tension fills the air as they take the stage, showcasing months of hard work before esteemed judges. The suspense peaks as winners are announced, celebrating triumphs in innovation, with special recognition for first-year and all-girls teams, inspiring a new generation of creators.
